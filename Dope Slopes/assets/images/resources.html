Additional Info

I made the logo at the top of each page next to the paragraph from scratch.

The embedded media is on the Weather Watch page. Its is a video that tracks a current storm.

The roll over effect in CSS is located on every image next to their respective resort names. The border changes colors.

The Java script I used on this site was for the background snow falling effect. I got help from an online source that I sited in the resources.

I am proud of this project because I chose a subject that I would enjoy working on. Although it took me way longer than I had planed to gather the content, it was worth it. I think the content, variety, and style are this site's best aspects.

I spent hours scouring through resources on the search for statistics, opinions, and best aspects of each venue. For each venue, I averaged their ratings/statistics for each category I rated them on, providing the most accurate ratings I could give. I ended up branching out to more types of snow venues than I thought I would such as backcountry, skiing specifics, and snowboarding specifics. This ended up making the target audience much broader resulting in more hits potentially. Lastly, I thought I did a good job on the style of the site. The humer and design appeal to the majority of riders whether ski or snowboard.

One thing I could improve on are my grid work. Sometimes it looks a little awkward, but I did my best to fix it. Another thing I could do is to add a couple more design concepts to spice the site up.
